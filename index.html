Frequently Asked Questions

Do you create completely custom designs?

Yes, every design is created from scratch based on your vision, preferences, and body type.

How long does it take?

The typical timeline is 4-8 weeks, depending on the complexity of the design and current workload.

Do you offer virtual consultations?

Absolutely! We offer virtual styling sessions via Zoom or WhatsApp for clients worldwide.

What if my measurements change?

We can make adjustments during the fitting process before final delivery to ensure the perfect fit.
